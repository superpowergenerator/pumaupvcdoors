Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Effective Date: 2023-10-01

At Puma Upvc Doors, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and address when you contact us or make a purchase.

We also collect non-personal information such as browser type, operating system, and pages visited to improve our website.

How We Use Information

Your information helps us to provide better services, process transactions, and communicate effectively with you.

We may use your contact details to send you updates about our products and services, but you can opt-out at any time.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. A cookie is a small file stored on your device.

You can manage cookie settings through your browser, but disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

Analytics and Third-Party Tools

We use analytics tools to monitor website performance and user behaviour, which helps us improve our services.

These tools may collect data such as your IP address and browsing habits, but we do not control these third-party tools.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted partners to help us operate our business.

Your information may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy.

When your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete it.

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws.

We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website.
